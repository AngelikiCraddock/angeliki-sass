Harvey Dale and the Cements

Meet the Band

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale has always loved singing. Growing up in the lively music scene of Austin inspired him to pursue a rockstar career. Once he met his bandmates in his junior year of highschool, the rest was history. Though talented in other instruments, such as the guitar, Dale is fascinated with exploring the fusion of vocals and electronic tunes.

Marcus Lorn

Marcus Lorn grew up in Austin and is a multi-talented instrumentalist.Though he play everything from the piano to the harp, he fell in love the bass. Always a fan of jazz his biggest inspirations for the bass are from the genre. However, Lorn enjoys experimenting with the bass in the punk genre.

Jimmy Page

Jimmy Page is an expert in all things strings. From the mandolin to the violin, Page never grows tired of learning new techniques and riffs.Once he met his equally talented bandmates he found a place he could explore and experiment. Sticking with his beloved electric guitar for stage performances, he appreciates the versatility of the punk/electronic genre when it comes to long solos.
